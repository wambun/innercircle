## About Page Content

### Owner Bio
**Gregory D. Hostelley, CPA, CGMA** celebrates 40 years in the accounting field. A lifetime Cleveland resident and St. Ignatius alumnus, Greg attended Baldwin-Wallace University and became one of the youngest accountants in Northeast Ohio.

### Company Story
**From Adding Machine Tapes to Trusted Business Advisor: The Inner Circle Advisors Story**

The firm originated when Greg's father opened his own accounting firm on April 16, 1970, originally focusing on the healthcare industry. Today, they have served thousands of clients in Northern Ohio and across the United States and Canada.
